About Me Personally


Growing up, I am the youngest out of all my siblings.
People always assume that I am the most spoil since I am the youngest, but that's not true.
Seeing all my siblings grow up, I try my best to not follow their footsteps and learn from all there mistakes.

I am not one of your typical Hmong women.
I love my culture and embrace who I am as a Hmong woman, but I can't follow the footstep of a Hmong woman.
One of the reasons is that I am Hmong-American. Growing up in Wisconsin, Milwaukee, my world expanded in the community I grew up.
I love that I grew up in Wisconsin, Milwaukee because here in Milwaukee the cultures are so diversere, and it amuses me because everyone was so different from me.

People say different is bad, because different to me, means change.
Change is bad to most because of not knowing what to expect.
I embrace different/change.
